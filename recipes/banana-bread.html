Banana Bread Recipe

Description

This banana bread recipe creates the most delicious, moist loaf with loads of banana flavor. this all recipes community facorite will quickly become your go-to banana bread recipe!!

Ingredients

  • Flour: All-purpose flour gives the banana bread structure
  • Baking Soda: Baking soda acts as a leavener, helps to rise
  • Salt: Pinch of salt enhances the overall flavor, dont go too overboard
  • Butter: Stick of butter lends rich, moisture, and irresistible flavor
  • Brown Sugar: sweetens things up
  • Eggs: Binding agent, hold the batter together
  • Banana: only overripe banana

Steps

  1. Combine the dry ingredients in one bowl
  2. Beat the butter and sugar in another bowl, add eggs and mashed banana
  3. Add the wet mixture to the dry mixture
  4. Pour the batter into loaf pan, pre-heat oven and bake it.
