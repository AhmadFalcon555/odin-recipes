Crunchy French Onion Chicken

Description

This bang bang sauce is a sweet and spicy mayonnaise-based sauce that is good on fried fish, chicken, fried potatoes â€” almost everything!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 Cups French-onions, crushed
  • 1 Large Egg, beaten / 2 small
  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Steps

  1. Gather all Ingredients
  2. Pre-heat the oven
  3. spread crushed onions in a shallow bowl. pour egg into a separate shallow bowl
  4. Dip chicken into beaten egg, then press into crushed onions, gently tap chicken to let loose pieces fall away, place breaded chicken on a baking sheet
  5. bake until no longer pink and juices run clear, about 20 minutes
